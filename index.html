What We Offer

Train Hard.
Train Smart.

Whether you're chasing PRs or just trying to survive burpees — there's a place for you here.

01
🏋️
Group Classes

Multiple sessions throughout the week built around CrossFit methodology. Come in, push hard, leave better. Every skill level welcome — coaches scale everything.

02
🎯
1-on-1 Training

Your goals, your pace. Work directly with an expert coach to build a program that fits your life and gets you where you want to go faster.

03
🥗
Nutrition Coaching

Supplement your training with professional nutrition guidance. Because what you do outside the gym matters just as much as what you do inside it.

04
⚡
Olympic Lifting

Safely learn the snatch, the clean & jerk, and accessory work from coaches who know their stuff. Build explosive power and real technique.

05
🧘
Yoga

New to the schedule — Tuesdays & Thursdays at 8 PM. Balance your intensity with mobility, breath work, and mental reset. Try the first two weeks free.

06
🤝
Community

The Box is a family. People who show up for each other on and off the floor. You won't find a more welcoming, encouraging gym in the valley.
